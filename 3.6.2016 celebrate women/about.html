ABOUT

CELEBRATE WOMEN is a photography series aimed at and focused around the empowerment of women. It is an open invitation photography project that asks participants, "what empowers you?" Each woman is photographed with the signifier(s) she brings, and it is an oppurtunity for her express and explore her identity as she wishes with the assistance of an experienced photographer. Participants exert as much creative control over their visual narratives as possible, choosing their poses, props, and the final image.


CELEBRATE WOMEN will be published on International Womenâ€™s Day, March 8th. It is a youth led initiative heavily motivated by Sustainable Development Goal number five: achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. CELEBRATE WOMEN addresses many issues including the lack of female representation in the art world, lack of varied female representation in the media, and the objectification and oversimplification of women.
